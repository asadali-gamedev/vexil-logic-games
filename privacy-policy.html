1. Introduction Protocol

Welcome to Vexil Logic Games. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency in how we handle your personal data when you play our mobile games (“Games” or “Services”). By downloading and using our Games, you confirm your acceptance of this policy.

This policy complies with:

  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) – For users in the European Economic Area (EEA) and the UK.
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) – For California residents.
  • Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) – For users under 13 years old.

2. Information Ingestion

2.1 Personal Data (Voluntary)
  • Name and contact info: If you facilitate support requests or create an account.
  • Payment information: Processed securely via providers (Google Play, Apple Pay). We do NOT store payment details locally.
  • User-generated content: Chat messages, profile data, or other info created in-game.
2.2 Non-Personal Data (Automatic)
  • Device data: Model, OS, IP address, advertising ID.
  • Gameplay metrics: Session times, in-game actions, feature interactions.
2.3 Device Permissions

We may request access to hardware for gameplay features. You can deny these in system settings:

  • Camera
  • Microphone
  • Storage

3. Processing Algorithms

We utilize your data to:

  • Improve the framework: Enhance features, debug code, and optimize fun.
  • Targeted Operations: Deliver personalized ads based on Device ID (opt-out available via settings).
  • Analytics: Understand user behavior patterns to upgrade future updates.
